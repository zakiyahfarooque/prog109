Space and Cowboy Bebop

Cowboy Bebop is a Japanese anime series set in a future where humanity has colonized the solar system. The series follows the adventures of a group of bounty hunters, known as cowboys, as they travel the galaxy in pursuit of dangerous criminals. The show is known for its iconic jazz-inspired soundtrack and blend of genres, including science fiction and film noir.
