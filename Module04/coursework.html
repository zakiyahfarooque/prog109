Name of the course: Introduction to Digital Photography

ART154

Professor: Chris Arslain

Course Description

What did you know before this class?

I knew how to take photos with my digital camera, and change the exposure. I was still pretty inexperienced though, and photography has always been something I have wanted to try.

What knowledge/skills did you gain after this class?

I learned all the settings of my camera, such as how to change the ISO, aperture, shutter speed, and more. I also learned how to edit photos in Photoshop and developed some film photography as well!
